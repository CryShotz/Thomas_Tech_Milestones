Tech Milestons

January of 1984, Apple came out with the Macintosh. Job's main target of sells was the 25 million workers who sat behind desks all day and the 11 million kids at college or highschool. Jobs had grand ambitions for the product.With Vangelis’ “Chariots of Fire” playing in the background, the Apple co-founder showcased a number of the Macintosh’s capabilities, from its ability to “talk” to a drawing application that was able to produce sophisticated images.
